Frequently Asked Questions

What is a AutoEye Inspection Vehicle History Report?
Millions of consumers rely on AutoEye Inspection Reports every day to help them decide whether or not to buy a used Vehicle. Each report contains information about whether the Vehicle in question has been in an accident, has any open recalls, previous owners, service history and much, much more.
Does AutoEye Inspection have a report for every vehicle?
Yes, AutoEye Inspection strives to provide comprehensive reports for a vast majority of vehicles. Our database is constantly updated to ensure accuracy and coverage. However, for very old or rare vehicles, some data points might be limited.
Who is a Vehicle History Report for?
A Vehicle History Report is invaluable for anyone considering purchasing a used car. It's also useful for sellers who want to provide transparency to potential buyers, and for current owners curious about their vehicle's past.
What is a VIN?
A VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) is a unique 17-character alphanumeric code assigned to every motor vehicle upon its manufacture. It serves as the car's fingerprint, as no two vehicles in operation have the same VIN.
